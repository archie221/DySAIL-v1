Guidelines

  • Search Bar is used to search the student by RollNo for updating his/her seat status

  • According to th current status of student's booking search bar redirects to corresponding page

  • The pages corresponding to each booking status are:

    1. Booked: Requests you to fill-in belonging status of student and then on confirming booking status goes to occupied

    2. Occupied: Shows you details regarding the student and allows you to change belonging status, idle seat or terminate booking

    3. Idle: Asks you for either terminating idling or terminate booking

  • On clicking Idle Seat you are redirected to a page where-in you are required to fill-in your tentative idling time

  • Overnight Idling is enabled from midnight till 6 A.M. allows you to idle your seat for 9 hours

NOTE

  • Keep changing the Belonging Status everytime student comes in or goes out of library
  • In case invalid RollNo is searched, page is not found
  • Booking N/A comes in case the student has not yet booked for a seat or is not able to make it in time
