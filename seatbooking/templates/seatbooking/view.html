WELCOME TO THE SEAT VIEWING FEATURE OF DySAIL


In order to book for a seat login

The seats are displayed in correspondence to their positioning in the library

The function associated with each type of seat is displayed on top of the mapping

HOVERING FEATURE :
You can hover over each seat to see its name
Booked and Occupied seats will also show the name of occupant(if not hidden)
Idle seats in addition to above will also show the tentative idled time

ONCLICK FEATURE :
Clicking vacant seats will lead to make them selected
Clicking Booked, Occupied and Idle seats will lead to the chatbox associated to the occupant
In case you are not able to click vacant seats then you may have a booked seat or, you have clubbed with other user as leader or a user clubbed with you has a booked seat

You can chat with a person sitting on any seat, the feature which can be used to ask for sitting on a idled seat

Inorder to not allow a person to know your identity on hovering you can use the Hide Identity Slider, which is by default set to False

Refresh to see the updated seat status, although every 30 seconds screen will refresh automatically
