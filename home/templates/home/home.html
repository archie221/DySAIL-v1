WELCOME TO DySAIL

Dynamic seat Allocation in Library


DySAIL acronym for Dynamic Seat Allocation in Library is quite a self explained name for a website. The solution to all problems of large population management, over a certain infrastructural resource, implemented in a flexible and user-friendly way. Centered basically on Library System, it consists of two user-interfaces, Student and Library Administration. Student Interface roots to the feature of viewing accurately and intricately mapped seats and their status in Library, and booking facility, which was incorporated owing to the fact of saving time and trouble of students in searching for seats. Whereas the root feature of Library Administration Interface coincides with the fact of managing large student check-in data in an efficient way
